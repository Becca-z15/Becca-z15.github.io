Financial Aid

The Financial Aid team is here to provide you with support and resources over the course of your studies. From OSAP, Travel Awards and Bursaries we want to make your experience the best! Have questions? Have a look around! I’m sure you’ll find what you are looking for.

The purpose of the FASA Loan is to provide short-term financial assistance for educational or living expenses to full-time or part-time students. There are maximum amounts of funds that the College can provide to students. In some cases, the student’s needs are in excess of the maximum which can be approved.
